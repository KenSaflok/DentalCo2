What is included in every plan

All plans include implementation guidance, patient payment link setup, access to the collections reporting dashboard, and support during your initial rollout. There are no per-transaction fees on patient payments.

What affects enterprise pricing

Location count, rollout timeline, reporting requirements, whether DentalCo manages membership migration from an existing system, and the level of API integration required with your practice management software.

Annual billing discount

Starter and Professional plans are available at a 15% discount when billed annually. Enterprise pricing can be structured quarterly or annually depending on rollout scope.
