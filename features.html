Platform features

Built for the dental revenue workflow — not generic billing software.

DentalCo gives operations leaders and office teams one system to manage patient balances, run membership programmes, and measure collections performance across every location.

A/R automation

Target the right patient balances at the right time.

Build reminder journeys by aging bucket, balance amount, payer type, or individual office rules. Reduce manual follow-up while keeping communication consistent across locations. Sequences adjust automatically as patients respond or balances change, so staff never need to re-sort a worklist by hand.

  • Configurable SMS and email sequences per aging bucket
  • Payer-type segmentation (insurance, self-pay, plan member)
  • Automatic de-escalation when patient pays or contacts office
  • Centralised sequence management for multi-location groups
Patient payments

Make it easier for patients to pay without a phone call.

Digital statements, payment plans, saved payment methods, and mobile-friendly checkout give patients faster ways to pay and staff fewer calls to handle. Clear, itemised statements reduce confusion and payment disputes before they start.

  • Mobile-first digital statement delivery
  • Saved cards for faster repeat payments
  • Flexible payment plan creation with automated charge scheduling
  • Real-time confirmation and receipt delivery
Membership system

Create recurring revenue from uninsured patients.

Launch custom membership plans, automate renewals, monitor failed payments, and track retention so plan growth never becomes an operational burden. Plans can be scoped to a single office or rolled out across the whole group with consistent pricing.

  • Custom plan builder with tiered benefit levels
  • Automated annual and monthly renewal cycles
  • Smart retry logic for failed charges
  • Member portal for self-service plan management
Analytics & reporting

See what is working across every office and location.

Measure collections lift, membership performance, overdue balance trends, and payment completion rates with dashboards built for operators and office managers alike. Export-ready reports give finance teams the data they need without custom SQL queries.

  • Consolidated collections dashboard across all locations
  • Overdue balance aging summary with drill-down to patient level
  • Membership KPIs: active plans, churn, failed charge rate
  • Scheduled report delivery to operations and finance teams
Multi-location controls

Consistent processes across every office without losing flexibility.

Corporate or operations teams can set company-wide policies for reminder timing, payment plan rules, and membership pricing — while individual offices retain the ability to add custom notes or adjust templates within approved boundaries.

  • Role-based access for corporate, regional, and office-level staff
  • Inherited configuration with office-level overrides
  • Consolidated billing performance benchmarks by location
  • DSO-ready rollout playbooks included
